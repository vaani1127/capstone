### Common Workflows

#### Workflow 1: Booking and Attending an Appointment

1. Book appointment with preferred doctor
2. Receive confirmation notification
3. Arrive at clinic on scheduled time
4. Check queue status on your phone
5. Wait for your turn (monitor queue updates)
6. Attend consultation when called
7. View consultation notes and prescription in Medical History after visit

### Troubleshooting

#### I forgot my password
1. Tap "Forgot Password" on login screen
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Follow instructions to create new password

#### I can't see my appointment
- Ensure you're logged in with the correct account
- Check if the appointment was successfully confirmed
- Try refreshing the appointments list
- Contact clinic staff if issue persists

#### Queue status is not updating
- Check your internet connection
- Close and reopen the app
- Ensure you have the latest app version
- Contact support if problem continues

#### I need to cancel but it's less than 2 hours before appointment
- Contact the clinic directly by phone
- Explain your situation to the staff
- They can manually cancel your appointment

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I book appointments?**
A: You can book appointments up to 30 days in advance.

**Q: Can I book appointments for family members?**
A: Each family member needs their own account. You can help them book using their account.

**Q: What if I'm running late for my appointment?**
A: Contact the clinic immediately. Your queue position may be adjusted based on availability.

**Q: Can I request a specific doctor?**
A: Yes, you can choose any available doctor when booking your appointment.

**Q: How accurate are the waiting time estimates?**
A: Estimates are based on average consultation times and update in real-time. Actual times may vary.

**Q: Can I download my medical records?**
A: Currently, you can view records in the app. Contact your doctor for official copies.

**Q: Is my medical data secure?**
A: Yes, all medical data is encrypted and protected using blockchain-inspired security technology.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I modify consultation notes after saving?**
A: Yes, but updates create a new version. Original notes are preserved for audit purposes.

**Q: How is my average consultation time calculated?**
A: The system uses a rolling average based on your recent consultations to estimate wait times.

**Q: Can I view other doctors' patient records?**
A: No, you can only view records for patients you are treating or have treated.

**Q: What if I make an error in a prescription?**
A: Update the prescription immediately. The system maintains version history for safety.

**Q: Can patients see my consultation notes?**
A: Yes, patients have read-only access to their complete medical records.

**Q: How do I handle emergency walk-ins?**
A: Coordinate with nursing staff to prioritize urgent cases in your queue.

**Q: Are my medical records legally compliant?**
A: Yes, all records are tamper-proof, auditable, and meet healthcare compliance standards.

## General Information

### System Security

HealthSaathi uses advanced security measures:
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Blockchain Integrity:** Medical records are protected using hash chaining
- **Tamper Detection:** Automatic detection of any data modifications
- **Audit Trail:** Complete, immutable log of all system activities
- **Role-Based Access:** Users only see data relevant to their role
- **Secure Authentication:** JWT tokens with expiration

### Data Privacy

- Patient data is confidential and protected
- Only authorized healthcare providers can access medical records
- Patients have full access to their own records
- All access is logged and auditable
- System complies with healthcare data protection regulations

## Glossary

- **Appointment:** Scheduled meeting between patient and doctor
- **Audit Chain:** Blockchain-inspired record of all system modifications
- **Check-in:** Process of patient arriving and joining doctor's queue
- **Consultation:** Medical examination and discussion between doctor and patient
- **Hash:** Cryptographic fingerprint used to detect tampering
- **JWT Token:** Secure authentication token
- **Medical Record:** Digital record of patient's medical history
- **Prescription:** Doctor's written order for medication
- **Queue:** Line of patients waiting to see a doctor
- **Queue Position:** Patient's place in the waiting line
- **Role:** User's permission level (Admin, Doctor, Nurse, Patient)
- **Tamper Detection:** System's ability to identify unauthorized data changes
- **Walk-in:** Patient who arrives without prior appointment
- **Waiting Time:** Estimated time until patient's consultation
