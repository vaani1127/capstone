## Citation Style

All references in this document follow **APA 7th Edition** style. For academic writing, please use proper citations:

**Journal Article:**
```
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of the article. Title of Periodical, volume(issue), pages. DOI
```

**Website/Report:**
```
Organization. (Year). Title of document. URL
```

**Book:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of book. Publisher. ISBN
```
